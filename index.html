FAQs

Explore our FAQs for fast, informative answers to frequently asked questions and common concerns.

How do I get started?
Sign up on vaulte.io, verify your email, deposit funds into Autopools (starting $10) or Vault (starting $1), and begin earning rewards.
How do Autopools work?
Autopools use an automated 2x2 matrix. You join Pool 1 for $10. From Pool 2 onwards, you earn 100% of the joining amount from members placed in your second level. Earnings from one pool help you upgrade to the next.
How does Vault work?
Vault allows you to stake USDT BEP20 (starting from $1) and earn up to 1.5% daily rewards. You can choose flexible staking (withdraw anytime) or fixed-term staking (higher rewards, locked period). Capital can be withdrawn anytime without fees.
Can I withdraw my funds anytime?
Yes. You can withdraw your staked capital from Vault anytime without charges. Autopool earnings and team bonuses can also be withdrawn anytime but incur a 10% fee.
What is the minimum deposit to start?
The minimum to start is $10 for Autopool 1 or $1 for Vault staking.
How do I earn from my team?
You earn team bonuses based on the staking activities (flexible or fixed) of members in your network, up to 7 levels deep. The larger your network, the higher your potential bonus rewards.
